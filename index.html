Curriculum Vitae

Coming from an interdisciplinary background, I have a great interest in the computational models of social constructs. So far, I have been in the intersection of cognitive science, affective computing, human-computer interaction, and social robotics for 5 years.

Currently, I am working as a PhD candidate researcher at Utrecht University. My project is on the development of an ontology for the consent lifecycle for human-AI collaborative systems, or hybrid intelligence! This project is a part of the Hybrid Intelligence Centre in the Netherlands, and is partially funded by the Dutch Research Council Gravitation Project grant.
